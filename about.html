About AnnieCodes

I completed SheCodes in 2024 and since then I have been passionate about working with brands and clients to deliver slick user-journeys. At AnnieCodes we believe that building a seamless user experience will deliver on sales and business KPIs.

We're passionate about building the best solutions for our clients and this is at the heart of everything that we do.

We have three core principles at AnnieCodes to ensure that we are delivering the best solutions in the most effective way for our clients. Firstly, we embed a culture of continuous improvement, making sure that we are testing and learning and making tweaks and builds where necessary. Secondly, we ensure that we are putting data at the center of our solutions, making sure we deliver the best possible journeys for customers and clients by tapping into insights from first party data. Lastly, we put relationships at the heart of what we do.
